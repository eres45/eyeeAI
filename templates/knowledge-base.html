Prevention Guidelines

Hand Hygiene

Wash hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds

Avoid Touching

Don't touch or rub your eyes with unwashed hands

Clean Environment

Maintain clean surroundings and avoid sharing personal items

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI diagnosis?

Our AI system has been trained on a vast dataset of eye conditions and achieves an accuracy rate of over 90%. However, it's important to note that this tool is meant for preliminary screening and should not replace professional medical advice.

How is my data protected?

We implement state-of-the-art encryption and security measures to protect your data. All images are processed securely and are not stored permanently. We comply with GDPR and HIPAA regulations to ensure your privacy.

What should I do after receiving a diagnosis?

If the system detects any eye condition, we recommend consulting with an eye care professional. The AI diagnosis serves as a preliminary assessment and should be confirmed by a medical expert.
